About Us

Aureolin Research, Consultancy, and Expertise Development (ARCED) Foundation is registered in Bangladesh under the Society Registration Act, 1860 at the RJSC and started its operation from 2013. ARCED Foundation collaborates with national and international organizations, agencies, institutions, and academicians to implement experimental, quasi-experimental and non-experimental researches.

Cutting-Edge Research

Most of the research and studies that ARCED carried out were methodologically embedded on Randomized Controlled Trial (RCT), quasi-experimental methods, cross-sectional design, Most Significant Changes for qualitative analysis, approach following either or mixture of simple random, stratified, clusters or systematic sampling.

Almost all of its studies involve statistical and econometrics tools and models using Stata, SPSS, Python or R along with Google Sheets, MS Excel and Access for basic data management. ARCED has part-time and full-time in-house professionals as well as a professional setup to secure experts from outside – that entails both national and international professionals.

What we are good at?

ARCED's expertise in Quantitative & qualitative research, real-time data collection. Baseline, assessment, evaluation, rapid appraisals. Agri-business, gender-based violence, health, nutrition, and more.

Digital Data Collection

Digital data collection enhances research through advanced survey tools, real-time monitoring, and agile programming, extracting valuable insights and optimizing metadata for informed decision-making.

Transparency & Reproducibility

Effective file management, ethical standards, code-based data processing, and automation ensure reliable research and transparent sharing of data and methods.

Data Privacy & Security

Ensure device security, data encryption, PII data processing, backup, and storage for safeguarding sensitive information and maintaining confidentiality

High Frequency Checks

Monitor survey progress, team performance, and conduct data error checks to ensure accuracy, efficiency, and reliable results.
